How It Works

Each artificial lifeform has unique DNA that determines its appearance (red variations with subtle gradients), behavior patterns, and survival traits. They move, breed, fight, and evolve in real-time based on their genetic code.

Life Mechanics

  • Breeding: Compatible lifeforms create offspring with inherited traits
  • Combat: Stronger lifeforms consume weaker ones
  • Evolution: DNA mutations create new characteristics
  • Gravity: When enabled, lifeforms fall downward like particles
  • Radiation: Higher levels increase mutation rates and reduce lifespan
  • Thanos Snap: Instantly removes half of all lifeforms for dramatic population control
